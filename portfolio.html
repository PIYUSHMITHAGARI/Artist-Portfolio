About

Priyanshu Mithagari is a passionate fine artist based in Alibag, Maharashtra, with a deep connection to the natural beauty of the Konkan region. His artistic journey began at a young age, and he has since developed a distinctive style that blends traditional techniques with contemporary sensibilities. Priyanshu's work reflects his fascination with the interplay of light, texture, and emotion, capturing moments of quiet beauty in both landscapes and portraits.

Artist Statement

My artistic practice is rooted in observation and emotional connection. I seek to capture not just what I see, but what I feel when confronted with a landscape or a person. Each brushstroke is a deliberate choice, each color a carefully considered emotion. My work in portraiture aims to reveal the inner world of my subjects, while my landscapes seek to preserve fleeting moments of natural beauty. I believe art should speak to our shared human experience, connecting us through visual storytelling.

Featured Paintings

Whispers of the Valley

An exploration of light and shadow inspired by morning fog over hills. Oil on canvas, 2024. The work captures the silence of early dawn using muted tones and soft texture, conveying a sense of tranquility and mystery.

Golden Horizon

A reflection on change and persistence, painted during sunset hours. Acrylic and gold leaf on wood panel, 2023. This piece explores the transient beauty of light at day's end, with layered textures creating depth and movement.

Silent Garden

Mixed-media piece featuring pressed flowers, pigments, and resin layers. It investigates memory through delicate organic forms, creating a dialogue between preservation and decay, past and present.
