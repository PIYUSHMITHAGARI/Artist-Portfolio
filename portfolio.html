Artist Statement

A dedicated fine artist with formal art education from Konkan, specializing in realistic landscapes and portraits. Brings emotion, precision, and storytelling into each artwork. Skilled in traditional techniques with a strong grasp of composition, detail, and color harmony. Work has been showcased in group exhibitions at renowned galleries like Jahangir Art Gallery and Nehru Art Gallery. Also completed commercial wall art projects, blending creativity with client vision. Passionate about exploring new themes rooted in nature and culture.Continuously learning and evolving to bring fresh perspectives to every canvas.

Featured Paintings

Whispers of the Valley

An exploration of light and shadow inspired by morning fog over hills. Oil on canvas, 2024. The work captures the silence of early dawn using muted tones and soft texture.

Golden Horizon

A reflection on change and persistence, painted during sunset hours. Acrylic and gold leaf on wood panel, 2023.

Silent Garden

Mixed-media piece featuring pressed flowers, pigments, and resin layers. It investigates memory through delicate organic forms.
